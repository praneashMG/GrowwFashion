Your Questions Answered

Browse through our most commonly asked questions or use the categories below to find specific information.

How long does shipping take? +

Shipping times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • International Shipping: 10-15 business days

You'll receive a tracking number once your order is shipped.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location. Please note that customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations.

How do I find my correct size? +

We provide detailed size charts for each product category. To find your perfect fit:

  1. Measure your bust/chest, waist, and hips
  2. Refer to the size chart on the product page
  3. Consider the garment's fit (slim, regular, or loose)
  4. Check customer reviews for sizing feedback

If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort.

Are your fabrics authentic and high-quality? +

Absolutely! We source only authentic, high-quality fabrics:

  • Silks: Pure Banarasi, Kanjivaram, Tussar
  • Cottons: Handloom, Khadi, Organic
  • Wools: Pashmina, Cashmere blends
  • Embroideries: Hand-stitched Zardozi, Aari, Phulkari

Each product description includes fabric details and care instructions.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for all regular-priced items:

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags
  • Customized items are non-returnable
  • Sale items can be exchanged for store credit
  • Returns are processed within 5-7 business days

To initiate a return, visit our Returns Center in your account.

How long do refunds take to process? +

Once we receive your returned items:

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Credit card refunds appear in 7-10 business days
  • PayPal refunds appear within 24 hours
  • Bank transfers may take 10-15 business days

You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund is processed.

How does the customization process work? +

Our customization process involves 5 simple steps:

  1. Consultation: Share your vision with our design experts
  2. Design Selection: Choose fabrics, patterns, and embroidery
  3. Measurement: Provide precise measurements (we guide you)
  4. Crafting: Master artisans handcraft your garment (2-4 weeks)
  5. Delivery: Worldwide shipping with tracking

Customized items cannot be returned unless there's a manufacturing defect.

How long does customization take? +

Customization timelines vary based on complexity:

  • Simple alterations: 7-10 business days
  • Standard customization: 2-3 weeks
  • Complex designs with heavy embroidery: 4-6 weeks
  • Bridal/wedding outfits: 6-8 weeks

We'll provide an estimated completion date during the consultation phase.

How do I care for traditional garments? +

Proper care ensures your traditional garments last for generations:

  • Silks: Dry clean only, store in muslin cloth
  • Cottons: Hand wash in cold water, line dry
  • Heavy embroideries: Professional dry cleaning recommended
  • Storage: Fold with acid-free tissue paper, avoid plastic
  • Zari work: Store away from moisture to prevent tarnishing
Do you offer gift wrapping and gift cards? +

Yes, we offer several gifting options:

  • Gift Wrapping: Available for $9.99, includes premium box and ribbon
  • Gift Cards: Available in denominations from $25-$1000
  • Personalized Notes: Include a handwritten note with your gift
  • Surprise Deliveries: We can ship directly to the recipient

Gift cards never expire and can be used online or in-store.
