The Evolution of Bridal Lehenga

From royal courts to modern weddings, discover how the bridal lehenga has transformed through centuries

2024

Mughal Influence

Introduction of intricate zari work, silk fabrics, and Persian motifs. Lehengas were heavily embroidered with gold and silver threads, worn by royal brides.

2026

Regional Variations

Different regions developed unique styles - Banarasi silk from Varanasi, Kanchipuram from South India, and Bandhani from Gujarat and Rajasthan.

2020

Colonial Impact

Introduction of new fabrics like velvet and incorporation of European lace. Lehengas became more structured with fitted cholis.

2021

Modern Fusion

Blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary designs. Lightweight fabrics, pastel colors, and fusion silhouettes become popular.
